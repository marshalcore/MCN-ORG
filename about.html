About Marshal Core of Nigeria

Committed to discipline, service, and national development

Who We Are

The Marshal Core of Nigeria was founded on March 19, 2025 as a registered and government-recognized voluntary organization. We are committed to empowering Nigerian youths through volunteerism, promoting discipline, and supporting national development through community service and patriotic engagement.

As a voluntary service organization under the National Youth Council of Nigeria, our mission is built on integrity, dedication, and selfless service to the nation.

Aims and Objectives

  • To promote social and economic development by engaging members in collaborative initiatives with government agencies and NGOs
  • To empower Nigerian youths by creating job opportunities and providing alternative employment pathways
  • To instill strong moral values, patriotism, volunteerism, and commitment to community service
  • To train and equip youths with skills in education, conflict resolution, and confidence-building
  • To assist in crowd control, neighborhood watch, and community security efforts

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Maintaining law and order
  • Protecting lives and property
  • Providing private security services
  • Creating job opportunities for citizens
  • Supporting federal government security efforts

Flag & Symbolism

The Marshal Core flag features a bold red and deep navy blue bisection, sharply divided diagonally, housing the organization's insignia at its heart.

Red — Courage, valor, and sacrifice. Reflects the bravery of every officer and passion to uphold justice.

Navy Blue — Discipline, loyalty, and authority. Represents structured command and honor.
