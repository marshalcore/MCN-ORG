Establishment of the Marshal Board Committee:
The Marshal Board Committee is hereby established as a section within the Marshal Core of Nigeria, responsible for recruitment/Enlisting, dismissing, and promoting officers of the Marshal Core of Nigeria.

Composition of the Marshal Board Committee: The Marshal Board Committee shall comprise of:

Federal government officers:

- Representatives from the Nigeria Police
- Representatives from the Nigeria Army/Air Force/Navy
- Representatives from the Nigeria Security and Civil Defense Corps
- Representatives from EFCC (Economic and Financial Crimes Commission)
- Representatives from DSS (Department of State Services)
- Representatives from Correctional Service/fire/immigration service

State government officers

- Retired/ serving Marshal Core of Nigeria officers who have served well and attained the rank of Assistant Director General of the Marshal Core of Nigeria.

Responsibilities:

The Marshal Board Committee shall be responsible for:
- Recruitment of officers into the Marshal Core of Nigeria
- Enlisting and promotion of officers
- Dismissal of officers
- Other matters related to the management of Marshal Core of Nigeria officers
This establishment is aimed at ensuring effective management and oversight of the Marshal Core of Nigeria.

Establishment of the Marshal Board of Trustees.

The Marshal Board of Trustees is hereby established to oversee the welfare and affairs of the National/State Headquarters of the Marshal Core of Nigeria.

Composition of the Marshal Board of Trustees:

The Marshal Board of Trustees shall comprise of Marshal Core officers holding the following ranks:
- Assistant Martial Officer I
- Deputy Superintendent of Marshal
- Superintendent of Marshal
- Assistant Director
- Deputy Director
- Director

Responsibilities:

The Marshal Board of Trustees shall be responsible for:
- Overseeing the welfare of the National/State Headquarters of the Marshal Core of Nigeria
- Managing the affairs of the National/State Headquarters
- Making decisions for the benefit of the Marshal Core of Nigeria
This establishment is aimed at ensuring the smooth operation and management of the National/State Headquarters of the Marshal Core of Nigeria.

DATA PROTECTION & PRIVACY POLICY

Marshal Board Committee (MBC) | Marshal Core of Nigeria

1. Introduction

The Marshal Board Committee (MBC) is a Federal Executive Body under the Marshal Core of Nigeria, responsible for appointing persons to offices and exercising disciplinary control. The MBC is committed to safeguarding the privacy, integrity, and security of personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Names and contact details
  • Date of birth and photographs
  • Academic records and employment history
  • Biometric data (facial recognition, fingerprints)
  • State/LGA of origin and next of kin details

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used for recruitment screening, candidate evaluation, aptitude testing, final selection, promotion, and disciplinary functions.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and physical measures to safeguard your information. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Contact Us

Data Protection Officer: info@marshalcoreofnigeria.ng

For data requests, consent withdrawal, or privacy concerns, please contact our DPO.
