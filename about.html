About

The Marshal Core of Nigeria

The Marshal Core of Nigeria was founded on March 19, 2025, by Director General Oseoboh Joshua Eromonsele of the Nigeria Police Force (NPF). The organization is structured into three key divisions:

  • Marshal Board Committee (MBC)
  • Marshal Board of Trustees (MBT)
  • Marshal Core of Nigeria (MCN)

The MBC and MBT are responsible for overseeing and directing the affairs of the organization.

Aims and Objectives

  • (a) To promote social and economic development by engaging members and volunteers in collaborative initiatives with government agencies, schools, non-profit organizations, and NGOs‚Äîaimed at productively engaging Nigerian youth.
  • (b) To empower Nigerian youths by creating job opportunities and providing alternative employment pathways.
  • (c) To instill strong moral values, patriotism, volunteerism, and a commitment to community service and nation-building among young Nigerians.
  • (d) To train and equip youths with skills in education, conflict resolution, reconciliation, and confidence-building.
  • (e) To offer youth-focused orientation and engage them in essential community services, including:
  • (i) Participation in public health initiatives such as immunization drives, census activities, and sanitation campaigns, especially in urban centers and local government capitals.
  • (ii) Distribution of refuse bins, wastebaskets, and portable waste bags in public facilities, buses, and schools to reduce environmental pollution.
  • (iii) Assisting in planting economic trees and engaging in environmental conservation programs.
  • (iv) Supporting crowd control during public events and contributing to neighborhood watch efforts to help improve community security.
  • (f) To establish a centralized database of trained, qualified professionals and non-professional youths, providing accessible manpower for national development.

Duties and Responsibilities of Marshal Core Officers

  • Maintaining law and order
  • Protecting lives and property
  • Providing private security services
  • Creating job opportunities for citizens
  • Supporting federal government security efforts in enforcing peace and order within society
