Our Capabilities

Operational & Support Units

The Marshal Core of Nigeria is organized into specialized units to effectively deliver on our mandate of protecting lives and property.

Operational Units

Security Operations Unit

Handles security duties, coordinates with law enforcement, patrols, and responds to incidents.

Disaster Response Unit

Responds to emergencies, provides relief and support, evacuation, and rescue operations.

Rapid Response Unit

Handles urgent situations, provides immediate response to threats or emergencies.

Community Protection Unit

Engages with communities, promotes safety, and protects vulnerable groups.

Traffic Management Unit

Manages traffic, ensures road safety, and coordinates with transport authorities.

Cyber Security Unit

Handles cyber threats, protects digital assets, and investigates cybercrimes.

Specialized Units

Environmental Protection Unit

Promotes environmental conservation, responds to environmental emergencies.

Youth Development Unit

Engages with youth, promotes skills development, and supports empowerment programs.

ICT/Tech Innovation Training

Empowers youth with digital skills including web development, cybersecurity, data science, cloud computing, and digital marketing.
