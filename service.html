Volunteer Services

Join Us in Making a Difference

At Marshal Core of Nigeria, we believe in the power of volunteerism to transform communities. As a voluntary service organization, we offer diverse opportunities for citizens to contribute to national development through meaningful engagement and community service.

Community Engagement & Outreach

Participate in community development programs that make a real difference:

  • Public health awareness campaigns
  • Environmental sanitation projects
  • Educational support programs
  • Community sensitization initiatives

Youth Development & Mentorship

Help shape the next generation of Nigerian leaders:

  • Leadership training workshops
  • Skills acquisition programs
  • Career guidance sessions
  • Moral and value-based education

Event Support & Crowd Management

Assist in maintaining order and safety during public events:

  • Public gathering assistance
  • Traffic control support
  • Event security volunteering
  • Crowd coordination services

Emergency Response Support

Be part of our emergency preparedness team:

  • Disaster response volunteering
  • First aid and emergency care
  • Relief distribution assistance
  • Crisis management support

Public Awareness & Education

Help spread important messages to the community:

  • Civic education programs
  • Patriotism campaigns
  • Security awareness initiatives
  • National unity advocacy

Administrative & Office Support

Contribute your skills to our organizational operations:

  • Office administration assistance
  • Data entry and management
  • Communication support
  • Program coordination help
